About KHU Enterprise

15 years of excellence in hosiery, sportswear & leather

Our Story

Founded in 2010, KHU Enterprise has grown from a small local supplier into an internationally recognised manufacturer and exporter of premium hosiery, sportswear, and leather goods — based in Lahore, Pakistan.

We serve over 5,000 clients across 150+ countries — from independent boutiques to large-scale retail chains and sports clubs in Europe, the UK, and the USA. Our team of skilled artisans and textile experts ensure that every product leaving our facility meets the highest international quality standards.

We operate as a direct-from-factory export brand. All orders are shipped internationally from Pakistan via DHL, FedEx, and freight partners. We do not operate a physical retail store in Europe, UK, or USA — we deliver straight to your door.

Whether you're a retail buyer looking for a premium leather jacket, or a business owner seeking reliable wholesale supply with OEM customisation — KHU Enterprise is your trusted partner.
